Privacy Policy for WeatherLook

Effective Date: December 10, 2025

Introduction

WeatherLook (“we”, “our”, or “the App”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, stored, shared, and your rights under privacy laws including GDPR (EU), CCPA (California, USA), and other applicable laws.

1. Data We Collect

  • Photos and thumbnails: Images uploaded by users.
  • Location data: GPS latitude/longitude and location names.
  • Weather metadata: Temperature, weather description, timestamp of upload.
  • Optional social handle: Only stored if entered by the user.
  • Public view count: Tracks how many times an image has been viewed.

No user accounts, email addresses, or personal identifiers are required.

2. How Data is Stored

  • Firebase Firestore: Stores metadata including image URLs, GPS coordinates, location names, timestamps, weather info, view counts, and optional social handles.
  • Firebase Storage: Stores full-size images.
  • On-device storage: Temporary files for image capture, compression, and caching.

3. How We Use Data

  • Display uploaded images and weather/location information in the app.
  • Maintain the public view count of images.
  • Support app functionality such as browsing and search.
  • Optional social handles are only used for attribution.

4. Data Sharing and Public Access

  • Uploaded images and metadata are publicly viewable by anyone using the app.
  • We do not sell, rent, or trade user data.
  • Third-party services such as AdMob and payment processors may process limited data for advertising and payments.
  • Ad consent for EU users: Personalized ads may be shown using device identifiers; users in the EU can manage ad preferences via their device or Google settings.

5. Third-Party Services

  • AdMob: Google may collect device identifiers for personalized ads.
  • In-App Purchases: Payments are handled by Google Play or Apple App Store; we do not store personal payment information.
  • See Google Privacy Policy and Apple Privacy Policy for details.

6. User Rights

GDPR (EU residents): Access, delete, or correct your uploads.

CCPA (California residents): Know what personal information is collected, request deletion, and opt out of data sales (we do not sell data).

Users in other regions may have additional rights under local privacy laws. Requests can be sent to our contact information below.

7. Data Retention

All uploaded images and metadata remain public unless removed by a moderator. Temporary files stored on devices are deleted automatically after use.

8. Moderation and Deletion

Users can contact us to request removal of images they have uploaded. Once verified, the images will be deleted from Firebase Storage and Firestore.

9. Security Measures

  • Data protected by Firebase security rules.
  • Only authorized updates are allowed for public metadata.
  • No personal accounts or authentication data is stored.

10. Children’s Privacy

WeatherLook is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under this age.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted in the app and the effective date will be revised.
